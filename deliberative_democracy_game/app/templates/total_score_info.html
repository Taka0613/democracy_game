How the Total Score is Calculated

The total score is calculated using a formula that takes into account the values for Environment, Economy, and Welfare. The score is determined as follows:

  • Base Score: The sum of Environment, Economy, and Welfare metrics.
  • Balance Bonus: An additional score is added if the metrics are balanced (i.e., the difference between the highest and lowest values is minimal).

This ensures that both the overall impact and the balance between the different metrics contribute to the final total score.
